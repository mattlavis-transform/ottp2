Have non-originating parts been sufficiently processed?

You have said that not all of the parts that make up your goods originate in {{ context.country_name }}. In order to qualify for preferential treatment, your non-originating parts need to have been sufficiently processed to count as originating.

Some operations are regarded as too minor to confer originating status to non-originating materials that are used in the production of your goods. These operations are referred to as 'insufficient processing' and are specific to each trade agreement.
